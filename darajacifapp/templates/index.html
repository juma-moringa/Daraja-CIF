Education Cause

Support of education cause that need urgent attention

Daraja Civic Initiatives Forum (DCIF) works with Ministry of Education to enhance equitable access to quality education. This involves establishment of coordination committees/mechanisms at sub-county, county and national level to discuss) issues for action by MOE, to share best practices, lessons and replicable strategies.

Featured Programs

Early Childhood Development

We deliver our mandate through research, learning facilitation, community mobilization, advocacy/policy engagements, capacity building, and service delivery. Daraja is a member of advocacy and professional networks/coalitions such as Kenya ECD Network and Elimu Yetu Coalition.

Education

Daraja has also participated in a number of policies forums & processes including:
i. APBET Policy development for low cost private schools located in the non-formal settlements.
ii. ECDE policy review Ministry of Education.
iii. Daraja has supported the legislation of the Child care Facilities Act 2017 for Nairobi city County

Governance and Civic participation

Daraja has a strong Governance structure led by a 10-member board of management, whose role is to advise on policy and strategic direction.Daraja also has a seasoned M&E department headed by a Monitoring, evaluation and learning officer. To support program activities is a pool of project officers, trainers, Community Resource persons.
